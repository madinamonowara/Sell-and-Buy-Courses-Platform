Museum of Ice Cream

I Scream! You Scream! We All Scream for Ice Cream!

  • A swimming pool filled with 11,000 pounds of (plastic) rainbow sprinkes!
  • Helium from sugary edibile balloons that will make you sound like a cartoonish chipmunk
  • Different themed rooms - most provide a small sample of something sweet

ColorFactory - An Interactive Art Museum

Color Galore!

  • 16 amazing rooms to explore
  • No need to worry about photos! Cameras are placed in popular photo areas & photos will be emailed to you right after.
  • Hidden surprises in every room. Keep your eyes peeled!

SPYSCAPE Museum

Test Your Spy Skills To The Limits

  • 7 interactive galleries. Unleash your super-spy!
  • Everyone recieves a personal identity brand which stores your scores to determine brainpower, personality, risk-tolerance, etc.
  • Coat checks and free lockers are available

Museum of Illusions

Can You Trust What You See?

  • A visual, sensory and educational experience.
  • Optical illusions your brain can't figure out.
  • Don't forget your camera!
